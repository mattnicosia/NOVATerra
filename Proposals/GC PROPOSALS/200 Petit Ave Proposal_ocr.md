## Notes and Clarifications:

1. **Pricing Validity:** This budget is valid for **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of issuance.
2. **Scope of Work:** The budget is based on a **white box** finish for both the residential and retail spaces, meaning walls will be primed, utilities will be stubbed, and floors will be left unfinished.
3. **Construction Type:** Pricing assumes **Type V wood-frame construction** with non-union/open shop labor.
4. **Work Hours:** All work is to be performed during **normal working hours**; overtime and after-hours work are excluded.
5. **Permits & Fees:** Permits, filing fees, expediting fees, and DOB filing costs are **not included** unless explicitly stated.
6. **Surveying & Layout:** Any **surveying, stakeouts, and as-built drawings** are excluded unless specifically noted.
7. **Temporary Utilities:** Temporary electric, water, gas, and internet for construction use are **not included**.
8. **Utility Connections:** Permanent connections, tap fees, and meter costs for **gas, water, sewer, and electric** are excluded.
9. **Sitework & Foundations:** Earthwork, dewatering, rock removal, and unforeseen subsurface conditions are excluded. Pricing assumes normal soil conditions.
10. **Retail Space White Box Scope:**
  - Drywall, primed walls, and ceiling
  - HVAC, electrical, and plumbing stubbed to the space
  - No flooring, finishes, or fixtures included
  - Entry storefront system included per base design
11. **Residential Unit White Box Scope:**
  - Framing, drywall, primed walls, and ceiling
  - Rough plumbing, electrical, and mechanical systems in place
  - No kitchens, no bathrooms, no finishes included
12. **MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing):** Rough-ins are included, but no final fixtures or trim-out work.
13. **Fire Protection:** Sprinkler system will be installed per code. **Shop drawings, permits, testing, and sign-off are included.**
14. **Elevator:** Pricing includes one (1) passenger elevator per architectural specifications.
15. **Facade & Envelope:** Assumes **brick veneer & panel system** with aluminum windows per base drawings.
16. **Roofing:** Assumes a **TPO roofing system**, warranty included per manufacturer specifications.
17. **Windows & Doors:** Pricing includes **aluminum windows and storefront system**. No window treatments or interior doors.
18. **Interior Partitions:** No demising walls inside residential units; all walls are assumed to be per fire separation and corridor design.
19. **Insulation & Soundproofing:** Includes **standard insulation per energy code**. Any enhanced soundproofing is excluded.
20. **Stairs & Railings:** Includes **two egress stairwells with code-compliant railings**.
21. **Common Areas:** Lobby and corridors **not included** in white box scope. Any finishes, millwork, or lighting beyond life safety requirements are excluded.
22. **HVAC:** Assumes **PTAC units** for residential spaces and a rooftop unit stubbed for the retail space.
23. **Parking & Sitework:** Any offsite work, sidewalk repairs, street trees, and DOT-related work are excluded.
24. **Exterior Lighting & Signage:** Excludes exterior signage and decorative lighting. Includes **code-required site lighting**.
25. **Sidewalk & ADA Compliance:** Sidewalk curb cuts, ramps, and ADA compliance-related work will be handled per municipal requirements but are excluded from this budget.
26. **Utility Relocation:** Any utility relocations, upgrades, or transformer installations required by the utility provider are excluded.
27. **Structural Requirements:** No major structural modifications or redesigns are included. Pricing assumes standard load-bearing conditions.
28. **Winter Conditions:** Any required **ground thawing, heating blankets, or winter additives for concrete** are excluded.
29. **Exclusions:**
  - No furniture, fixtures, or equipment (FF&E)
  - No appliances, cabinetry, or countertops
  - No security, AV, or access control systems
  - No specialty coatings or decorative treatments
30. **Change Orders & Contingency:** Any scope modifications or unforeseen conditions will be addressed via a formal change order process.
