# NOVATerra — Marketing Rollout Plan
**Created: February 25, 2026**

---

## PHASE 1: "Building in Public" (Weeks 1-4, Cost: $0)

- LinkedIn 3-5x/week — founder-led content about estimating pain points
- First "Speed Estimate" challenge video — "Can NOVA estimate this building in 5 minutes?"
- Private Slack community: "BLDG Estimators" (community-first, not product-first)
- Engage in r/estimating, Contractor Talk, BiggerPockets — provide value, don't sell
- Key insight: ZERO dedicated construction estimating influencers on LinkedIn — own this space

## PHASE 2: Waitlist + Invite-Only Beta (Months 1-3, Cost: $500-2K)

### "Founding Estimator" Program
| Tier | Limit | Price | Perks |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founding Estimator | First 50 | 50% off for life | Name on "Built By" page, direct Slack, roadmap votes |
| Early Adopter | Next 150 | 30% off first year | Priority support, early features |
| Beta Tester | Next 300 | Free during beta | Beta features, feedback channel |

- 8-email nurture sequence before access
- Cohort onboarding (10-15 at a time, like Linear's $400M playbook)
- First 50 users: mandatory 15-min 1-on-1 calls (like Superhuman)
- 3 invite codes per Founding Estimator (social currency)
- Every export says "Powered by NOVA" — viral loop

## PHASE 3: Content Engine + Live (Months 2-4, Cost: $0-2K)

- LinkedIn Live bi-weekly — demo with guest estimators (24x more comments than posts)
- "Estimate This Building" social series — post photo, crowd guesses, reveal NOVA ROM
- AI-generated cinematic product video (Runway + Sora + screen recordings, $100-500)
- Weekly Zoom estimator roundtables — 30 min open discussion

## PHASE 4: Guerrilla Tactics (Months 3-6, Cost: $500-3K)

- "Estimator's Survival Kit" mailer to 100-200 target GCs ($15-30/unit)
- Competitor event ambush — host parallel events near Procore/STACK conferences

- Pain-point content > product demos
