# BLDG Omni — Competitive Landscape
**Created: February 25, 2026**

---

## TIER 1: ENTERPRISE PLATFORMS

### Procore (NYSE: PCOR)
- Revenue: $1.3B (FY2025) | Market Cap: ~$8.1B
- Pricing: $375+/mo platform; Esticom estimating ~$99-139/user/mo
- 2,710 customers above $100K ARR, 95% gross retention
- Acquired Esticom (2020) for estimating — not native DNA
- Procore AI launched Jan 2025 (predictive analytics)
- **Weakness:** Estimating is bolted-on, not core. Pricing prohibitive for SMBs.

### Autodesk Construction Cloud
- AEC Revenue: $2.94B (FY2025) | Total: $6.13B
- Products: Autodesk Takeoff + Estimate (new) + ProEst ($5K/yr)
- 7.79M total subscriptions, massive BIM ecosystem
- **Weakness:** Fragmented (3 separate estimating products, different UIs). Enterprise pricing.

### Sage Estimating (Timberline)
- ~15,000+ companies on Sage 300 CRE | Pricing: $365/user/mo
- Deep cost database (RSMeans), accounting integration
- **Weakness:** On-premise legacy, dated UI, slow innovation. Ripe for disruption.

## TIER 2: MID-MARKET

### STACK Construction Technologies
- Revenue: $31.8M (2024), 54% YoY growth | Funding: $29.3M
- Pricing: $1,899/yr | Target: Small-to-mid GCs
- **Most direct mid-market competitor.** AI features still early-stage.

### PlanSwift (ConstructConnect)
- Pricing: ~$1,749/yr | 50,000+ materials database
- **Weakness:** Frequent crashes, controversial subscription shift, frustrated user base.

### Bluebeam Revu (Nemetschek)
- 3M+ users | Pricing: $260-440/yr
- Highest-rated on Capterra (4.7/5) but it's PDF markup, not estimating
- **Bluebeam Max (AI) launching early 2026** — watch closely

### Buildxact
- Revenue: ~$10.7M | Funding: $33M | Pricing: $133-439/mo
- **Best residential competitor.** AI Estimator Calculator, Home Depot integration.

## TIER 3: AI-NATIVE COMPETITORS

### Togal.AI
- Revenue: ~$2.2M, 333% YoY growth | Funding: ~$22.65M
- Auto-detects/labels/measures spaces in seconds | 4,000+ users
- **Takeoff ONLY — no full estimating.** 25 of ENR Top 400 as customers.

### Attentive.ai
- Funding: $49.7M ($30.5M Series B, Nov 2025, led by Insight Partners)
- 1,200+ customers | "Outcomes as a service" model
- **Service model (24-72hr turnaround), not self-service software.**

### Kreo Software
- Revenue: ~$2.4M | Team: ~22 (possibly downsizing)
- AI auto-measurement, cloud-based, affordable
- **Appears to be struggling operationally.** Not a major threat.

## KEY GAPS NO COMPETITOR FILLS
1. AI-powered schedule-to-estimate pipeline (BLDG Omni unique)
2. Historical proposal calibration (BLDG Omni unique)
3. Unified AI experience (not fragmented modules)
4. SMB-accessible AI estimating
5. Multi-schedule intelligence (9 types simultaneously)
6. Preconstruction knowledge graph from accumulated data
