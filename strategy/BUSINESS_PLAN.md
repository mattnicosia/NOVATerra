## COMPETITIVE POSITION

BLDG Omni occupies an **empty quadrant**: AI-native + full estimating. No competitor is here.

| | AI-Native | Legacy/Minimal AI |
|---|---|---|
| **Full Estimating** | **BLDG Omni (ALONE)** | Sage ($365/mo), Procore ($375+/mo), STACK ($1,899/yr) |
| **Takeoff Only** | Togal.AI ($22M raised), Attentive.ai ($49M raised) | Bluebeam ($260-440/yr), OST |

### Unique Advantages
1. 9-schedule-type AI parsing (no competitor does this)
2. Historical proposal calibration (tuned to YOUR costs, not industry averages)
3. Scan-to-ROM pipeline (plan upload → rough estimate in one workflow)
4. 3D building visualization from plan sheets
5. All-in-one: takeoff + estimating + AI + 3D in single interface
