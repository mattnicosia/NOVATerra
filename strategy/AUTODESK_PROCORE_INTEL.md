## AUTODESK — WHAT THEY'RE BUILDING

### Shipped/Beta
- **Autodesk Estimate** — new cloud estimating in ACC (announced AU 2025, pre-GA)
- **Autodesk Assistant** — AI natural language queries across RFIs, submittals, specs (beta)
- **Symbol Detection AI** in Takeoff — trace one symbol, AI counts all matching
- **AI Specifications Tool** — breaks spec books into searchable sections
- **Neural CAD** — generative AI for geometry (pre-beta, since 2020)

### Strategy
- Consolidating ACC into Forma (multi-year effort)
- Pipeline: BuildingConnected (bid) → Takeoff (quantify) → Estimate (price) → Build Cost Management
- ProEst ($5K/yr) will eventually be superseded by Autodesk Estimate
- 170+ incremental releases in 9 months, but big AI features still pre-beta

### Speed Assessment
- Shipping steadily but NOT aggressively on AI
- Most ambitious features (neural CAD, agentic assistants) are pre-beta
- Platform consolidation (ACC → Forma) is slowing competitive agility
- 9% workforce reduction in 2024 impacted velocity

### NOT Building
- ❌ Multi-schedule-type parsing from drawings
- ❌ Historical proposal calibration
- ❌ Scan-to-ROM pipeline
- Their AI is horizontal (search, summarization, symbol counting), not vertical estimating
